# Lesson 09 Extensions

## Co ban

1. Thu bo `live-source=True` va ghi lai hanh vi timestamp/batching.
2. Doi `fakesink` thanh sink hien thi neu muon xem live output.
3. In them thong tin pad/caps trong `cb_newpad(...)`.

## Nang hon nhung van trong boundary lesson

1. Truy cap sau hon vao `rtspsrc` de nghien cuu latency.
2. Them source RTSP thu hai sau khi ban da ro source bin mot nguon.
3. Ghi lai bang loi cua ban source bin dang "che giau" nhung doan nao cua pipeline.
